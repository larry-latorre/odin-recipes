Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of flat pasta sheets, typically alternating with rich and flavorful ingredients such as minced meat (usually beef or pork), tomato sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese. The layers are stacked and baked to perfection, resulting in a hearty and satisfying casserole. Lasagna is known for its comforting and indulgent nature, making it a popular choice for family gatherings and special occasions. The dish often incorporates a blend of savory spices and herbs, creating a delicious harmony of flavors in each bite.
